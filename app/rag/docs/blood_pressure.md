Title: Blood Pressure Categories
- Normal: <120/<80 mmHg.
- Elevated: 120–129 systolic and <80 diastolic.
- Stage 1: 130–139 systolic or 80–89 diastolic.
- Stage 2: 140–180 systolic or 90–120 diastolic.
- Hypertensive crisis: >180 systolic or >120 diastolic — seek urgent care.
- Lifestyle: lower sodium, regular activity, healthy weight, limit alcohol.
