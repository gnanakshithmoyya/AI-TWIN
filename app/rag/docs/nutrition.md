Title: Nutrition Context
- BMI: 18.5–24.9 is general healthy range; <18.5 underweight; 25–29.9 overweight; >=30 obesity.
- Vitamin D: deficient <20 ng/mL; insufficient 20–29; sufficient >=30.
- Vitamin B12: deficient <200 pg/mL; borderline 200–299; adequate >=300.
- Iron (ferritin): low <30 ng/mL may suggest iron deficiency; very high >400 needs medical review.
- Changes to labs should be guided by a clinician; this is not diagnostic advice.
